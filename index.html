Our Story

Where Technical Mastery Meets Creative Vision

Founded in 2012, Hamel Photo began with a simple belief: corporate photography shouldn't feel corporate. Every brand has a story worth telling beautifully.

Our team of photographers, cameramen and producers brings a combined 80+ years of experience across advertising, editorial, and documentary work. We approach every project with fresh eyes and meticulous attention to detail.

Based in Montreal, we work with clients across Quebec and Ontario, including long-term partners in Toronto, and we're always ready to travel wherever the project calls for it.

Startups, established corporations, non-profits, and everything in between: if you have a story, we'll tell it well.

— Jo Hamel
Founder & Creative Director
